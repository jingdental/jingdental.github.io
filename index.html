Meet Our Experienced Dentist

Dr. Jing Zhang

Dentist

Dr. Jing Zhang is the principal dentist of Jing Dental Clinic. She graduated with honors from the School of Stomatology, Beijing Medical University in 1994 and obtained her degree of bachelor of medicine. In the same year she was accepted into graduate school specializing in orthodontics. After 4 years of full time training with Professor Minkui Fu as the main tutor, Dr. Jing Zhang was rewarded her Ph. D degree in 1998. Before immigrating to Canada in 2009, she worked at the department of orthodontics, School and Hospital of Stomatology, Beijing Medical University (now Health Science Centre, Peking University). During her postgraduate program and ten plus years of clinical practice, Dr. Zhang treated thousands of patients with satisfactory results.

After passing series exam of the NDEB (National Dental Examining Board of Canada) in 2012, Dr. Zhang got her licence and began her practicing as a general dentist in great Toronto area.

In 2020, after 7 years of practice as an associate dentist in GTA, Dr. Zhang opened her own dental clinic. Alongside her more than 25 years of experience in orthodontics, she now provides full assessment of client’s oral condition and regular dental treatment which includes filling, cleaning, teeth extraction, root canal treatment and crown. She will continue to serve every single client with her constant enthusiasm, passion and skill.

Dr. Zhi Huang

Dentist

Dr. Zhi Huang graduated from the 8-year program at Peking University to obtain his Doctor of Dental Surgery degree. After graduation, Dr. Huang stayed at the dental school as a full-time faculty. After 1 year of training in advanced general dentistry followed by 3-year prosthodontics specialty residency, he became an assistant professor at the Department of Prosthodontics, Peking University School of Stomatology.

Dr. Huang has published clinical research articles in well-known international journals. After immigration to Canada in 2017, he started practising general dentistry.

Dr. Huang would like to devote himself in promoting oral and overall health for his patients using his knowledge and skills.

Dr. Xiaoxia Sun

Dentist

Dr. Xiaoxia Sun graduated from West China University of Medical Sciences and received her bachelor's degree and master's degree in Dentistry in 2003 and 2006. After graduation, she worked in Department of Endodontics of Tianjin Stomatological Hospital. She passed the NDEB Examinations and became a certified dentist in Canada in 2019.

Dr. Sun is a responsible, hardworking dentist, especially well experienced in root canal treatment.she is committed to providing high quality patient care.
