Meet Our Experience Dentist

Dr. Jing Zhang

Dentist

Dr. Zhang graduated with honor from the school of stomatology, Beijing Medical University in 1994 and obtained her degree of bachelor of medicine. In 1998, she was awarded Ph. D degree. Since then, she had been working as an orthodontist at the department of orthodontics, school and hospital of stomatology, Beijing Medical University (now Health Science Centre, Peking University) until immigrating to Canada in 2009. During her training in postgraduate program and clinical working, Dr. Zhang had treated thousands of patients with satisfactory results.

After passing series exams of the NDEB (National Dental Examining Board of Canada) in 2012, Dr. Zhang began her practicing as a general dentist in great Toronto area. Dr. Zhang is a member of Ontario Dental Association and World Federation of Orthodontists.
